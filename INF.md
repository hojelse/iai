If-Then-Else Normal Form (INF)
|               |INF                                 |
|:-:            |:-:                                 |
|$a$            |$a \to 1, 0$                        |
|$\neg a$       |$a \to 0, 1$                        |
|$a \land b$    |$a \to (b \to 1, 0), 0$             |
|$a \lor b$     |$a \to 1, (b \to 1, 0)$             |
|$a \implies b$ |$a \to (b \to 1, 0), 1$             |
|$a \iff b$     |$a \to (b \to 1, 0), (b \to 0, 1)$  |
|$a \oplus b$   |$a \to (b \to 0, 1), (b \to 1, 0)$  |
